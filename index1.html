Features

planning And organization

1:Planning: Business management involves setting goals, creating strategies, and developing plans to achieve organizational objectives. This includes short-term and long-term planning. 2:Organizing: This feature involves arranging and structuring resources, such as human, financial, and physical, to optimize efficiency and productivity. It also includes defining roles and responsibilities within the organization.

Decision-Making

Decision-Making: Business managers are responsible for making critical decisions that impact the organization. These decisions range from day-to-day choices to strategic, long-term plans.Decision making can be influenced by various factors, including personal values, past experiences, and emotional states.In a business context, data-driven decision making is becoming increasingly important, as it relies on quantitative analysis to guide choices and strategies.

Human Resource Management

Human Resource Management: Managing employees is a fundamental aspect of business management. It includes recruiting, training, performance evaluation, and addressing employee concerns.
